The Story

From Dust to Nashville

Hailing from the sun-baked backroads of rural Texas, Richard Dixen carried the grit and honest ache of the American heartland in every note he ever sang — but kept those songs locked in a dusty notebook for fifteen years.

When his marriage ended and his world came apart, Richard packed one worn suitcase and drove straight to Nashville. The songs that had been too raw to share became an album. The heartbreak became music.

Richard Dixen is the sound of a man who lost everything, only to find his voice.
